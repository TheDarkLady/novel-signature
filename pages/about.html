Our Story

Novel Office is known for offering the most exquisite office spaces in the commercial real estate market, seamlessly combining creativity, functionality, and innovation. First incorporated in 2008, we are a part of the Novel Group, established in 1993 in Dallas, TX, USA. Our reputation for delivering customized layouts with zero material wastage has set a new industry standard.

Now, Novel Signature Homes is set to redefine residential property development by constructing bespoke villas and single-unit luxury homes featuring open-concept designs with the same expertise and enthusiasm. Each residence showcases our unwavering commitment to excellence, meticulously designed to cater to the unique preferences and needs of discerning homeowners.

Driven by a deep passion for creating homes that are more than just living spaces but also experiences, Novel Signature Homes is entering the residential market with a clear vision: creating exclusive retreats. Located in prime locations, our homes offer exceptional curb appeal and are equipped with the latest smart home technology to enhance your living experience. From the initial planning stages to the final touches, each home embodies elegance, innovation, and sustainability. We are committed to inspiring you while promoting sustainable practices, ensuring that your new home is as responsible as it is luxurious.

Our Vision
